Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

Westwood Tech LLC, doing business as LuckyWildCatSoftware ("we," "us," or "our"), operates the Daily Lark mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to improve the App and deliver advertisements:

  • Analytics Data. We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as session duration, screens viewed, and general interaction patterns. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.
  • Advertising Data. We use Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and general usage data to serve ads. These ads are not targeted based on personal profiles or behavioral tracking.
  • Device Information. We may collect general device information such as device type, operating system version, and language settings for compatibility and analytics purposes.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Names, email addresses, or other personal contact information
  • Location data
  • Photos, contacts, or other on-device content
  • Financial or payment information

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Understand general App usage patterns and improve functionality
  • Display non-targeted banner advertisements
  • Monitor App performance and stability

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained by Google in accordance with their standard data retention policies. We do not maintain a separate database of user information.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Choices

  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking).
  • You may limit analytics data collection by disabling analytics in your device settings where available.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited data collected through the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
